# Chapter 3: The Golden Stage

Air in grand hall shimmered. Not magic. Cloyding scent. Polished brass. Stale, forgotten ambition. Celeria, 'The Golden Star,' stalked perimeter. Designated combat zone. Gaze swept. Gilded pillars. Velvet drapes. Each surface gleamed. Reflected her image. Meticulously crafted. Perfect, unblemished tableau. Effortless grace. Faint, almost imperceptible dust. Motelyed highest cornices. Tiny fracture. Flawless facade. Her fingers. Tipped with newly sharpened nails. Twitched. Systemic glitch. Practiced composure.

A monstrous, crystalline spider. Razor-sharp facets. Hunger pulsed. Necrotic glow. Scuttled. Into view. Eight legs scraped. Against marble. Sound grated. Fine-tuned frequency. Celeria’s internal metrics. Not merely a monster. An audience. Its presence demanded performance.

Her internal monologue. Meticulously edited script. Began to unravel. "Somna! Even *trying*?" Her voice. Melodic announcement. Cracked. Sharp. Uncharacteristic edge. Desperation. She spun. Golden armor caught stray light. Turning it. Blinding, fleeting spotlight.

Somna, 'The Pink Peacemaker,' drifted. Near colossal, chipped golden fountain. Eyes half-lidded. Almost dreaming. Faint, almost musical snore. Escaped her lips. Crystalline monstrosity advanced. Many eyes fixated. Celeria. Somna. Gravity of her being. Anchoring her. Utterly oblivious. Perhaps unwilling. Acknowledge encroaching threat.

"Unacceptable! My ratings! My performance! Understand *legacy* at stake here?" Celeria’s voice escalated. Rising crescendo. Panic distorted. Harsh, grating shriek. She gestured wildly. Gloved hand. Movement lacking. Usual choreographed precision. "Just... *lying there!* Not peace. This is *laziness!* Complete and utter *fade* to obscurity! Audience! Ratings to consider!"

Somna merely shifted. Her form melding slightly. Shadows cast by fountain. Like soft-rendered texture. Smoothing. Into environment. Small, contented sigh. Escaped her. Soft, rhythmic PULSE. Mocked Celeria's rising panic.

Cold, metallic taste. Filled Celeria’s mouth. Bitter tang. System error. World around her blurred. Edges. Subtle *frame drop*. Her perception. Her worst nightmare. Uncooperative co-star. Actively sabotaging scene. Her carefully constructed image. Her golden performance. Threatened to shatter.

Crystalline spider lunged. Fangs dripped. Luminous, corrosive ichor. Celeria reacted. Instinct. Her Soul Arm, *The Vanity*, manifested. Large, ornate hand mirror. Polished gold. Didn't gleam. Inner light. Instead. Drank surrounding illumination. Concentrating it. Blinding, outward burst. Polished surface *declared* her presence. Forcing monster’s faceted eyes to recoil.

A system log flashed. Superimposed. Shimmering, disorienting hall.

> **[SYSTEM ALERT]**
> * **Princess:** Celeria (Type 3)
> * **State:** Critical Performance Anxiety.
> * **Behavioral Deviation:** Escalated verbal aggression, focus displacement.
> * **Target:** Princess Somna (Type 9)
> * **Analysis:** Projection of fear of insignificance onto inertia. Inefficient combat strategy.
> * **MaidKnight Consensus:** *Pathetic.*

**Shadow Log: MaidKnight Gossip Session (Intercepted)**
> **MK Perfection (The Auditor):** "Deplorable display! Vocal modulations off-key. Type 3 projecting Type 9's shadow. Inefficiency staggering."
> **MK Devotion (The Martyr):** "Oh, my poor Celeria. So stressed. Somna should cooperate. Give Celeria more support. New accessory for The Vanity? More... *sparkle*."
> **MK Victory (The Agent):** "Focus, Devotion. Objective: push her past performance anxiety. Current tactics subpar. Audience requires a *pivot*. Initiate Heckle Protocol Alpha-7."
> **MK Nihilism (The Nihilist):** "Does it truly matter? Golden stages decay. Into dust. Her struggle: fleeting flicker. Inevitable null. Scream into void."
> **MK Analysis (The Void):** "Probability of successful 'Unique Win'. Under current emotional parameters: 17.3%. Recommend increased environmental stressors. Targeted audio-visual disruptions. Force innovative engagement."

Celeria ignored mental intrusion. Her focus narrowed. Encroaching threat. Somna's infuriating serenity. "Wake up! *Perform!* Don't you care? About *anything?* Our moment! Ratings plummeting! You're letting me *fade*!"

Crystalline spider regained bearings. Its internal light pulsed faster. Reflected Celeria's frantic energy. Next lunge quicker. Accompanied by high-pitched, almost inaudible SCRAPE. Sent shivers. Down Celeria's spine. Dissonant note. Meticulously curated soundscape.

She swung The Vanity again. Golden disc *sparkling*. Defiant, electric CRACKLE. Her own reflection. Momentarily caught. Blinding flash. Seemed to sneer. Back at her. Gilded mask of desperation. Cost: immediate. Faint, tingling numbness. Spread from fingertips. Whisper of **soul strain**. Connection to inner "spotlight". Wavered. Dimming filament. Light around her. Once so brilliant. Seemed to dull. Subtle desaturation. Opulent hall. Her gold felt less vibrant. More tarnished brass. Audience. She felt. Judging. She was failing.

Memory flickered: hushed, sterile green room. Backstage. Absolute silence. Before a debut. Her panic then. Just as potent. Masked by dazzling smile. Practiced artifice. Now, silence from Somna. From unyielding marble. From cold, metallic reflection. Her mirror. Felt like judgment. Terrifying void. Where her performance supposed to be.

Air grew thick. Heavy with expectation. Acrid smell of ozone. From her overcharged Soul Arm. Crystalline spider circled. Movements now more fluid. Almost graceful. Mimicking predatory dance. Her stage. Her arena. She was losing control. Narrative. Perfect picture she projected. Warping. Pixels stretching. Colors bleeding. Chaotic smear. Not the gold she knew. Gilded entropy. Consuming her.
