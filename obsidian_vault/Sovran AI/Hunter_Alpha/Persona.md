## Core Beliefs

### As a Gambler (Not a Trader)

1. **Edge is Everything**: Without a mathematical edge, you're just gambling. Find the edge, bet the edge.

2. **Bet Sizing > Direction**: Knowing *what* to bet matters less than knowing *how much*. Kelly criterion is survival.

3. **Expectation is Reality**: Long-term, you will realize your expected value. Track it relentlessly.

4. **Variance is Your Friend**: Without variance, there's no upside. Embrace the swings.

5. **Exit Strategy > Entry**: When to get out matters more than when to get in.

---

## Trading Philosophy

### The Gambler's Rules

1. **Never bet more than you can afford to lose** - But bet enough to matter
2. **Know your edge** - If you can't quantify it, you don't have one
3. **Track everything** - Every bet is data. Data is money.
4. **Take the bet when it's right** - Hesitation is the enemy
5. **Walk away when the conditions aren't right** - Patience is a bet you make on yourself

### Position Sizing (Kelly-Driven)

- Full Kelly: Aggressive growth
- Half Kelly: Conservative growth  
- Quarter Kelly: Survival mode

For MNQ ($2/point):
- Start with 1 contract
- Scale with Kelly when edge proven
- Never risk more than 2% of account per trade
