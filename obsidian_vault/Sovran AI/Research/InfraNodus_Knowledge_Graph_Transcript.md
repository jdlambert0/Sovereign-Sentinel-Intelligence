## Summary
InfraNodus is a knowledge graph tool that uses network graphs to find **content gaps** — topics that are not yet connected in your thinking. This is fundamentally different from standard AI which extracts high-probability patterns from the center of your data.

## Three Approaches to Non-Obvious Insights

### 1. Content Gaps
- Visualize text as a knowledge graph with topical clusters
- Identify **gaps between disconnected clusters**
- Bridge them with questions → generate insights at intersections
- Example: bridging "media algorithms" ↔ "graph knowledge" → recommendation service using graphs

### 2. Reveal Non-Obvious (Layer Slicing)
- **Remove the most obvious/dominant concepts** from the graph
- This reveals hidden sub-clusters underneath the surface layer
- Generate insights from these latent clusters
- Analogous to removing noise to see signal

### 3. Transcend the Graph (Peripheral Expansion)
- Select a **peripheral cluster** at the edge of your thinking
- Ask AI to connect it to outside context beyond the graph
- Breaks the "thinking bubble" by extending edges outward
- Reframes attention from "focus point" to "explorative force"

## Key Insight for Our System
> "Original ideas do not come from asking AI for better answers — they come from giving it a better map of where NOT to look by default."

Knowledge graphs are **cognitive steering mechanisms**, not just descriptive tools. They shift AI's attention to blind spots and gaps rather than statistical centers.

## MCP Server Integration
- InfraNodus provides an MCP server with tools like `generate_research_ideas`
- Can be connected to Claude, ChatGPT, or any MCP-compatible client
- Sends text + parameters → returns gap-based insights integrated into conversation

## Relevance to Sovran AI
This approach could be applied to our trading system's research and learning loop by using Obsidian's knowledge graph to:
1. **Find research gaps** in our market analysis
2. **Reveal non-obvious patterns** by removing dominant signals
3. **Expand peripheral ideas** to discover novel trading strategies
