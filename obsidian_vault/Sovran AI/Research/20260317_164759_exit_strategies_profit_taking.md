# Exit Strategies Profit Taking

### **Research Summary: Exit Strategies – Profit Taking**  
**Focus:** MNQ (Micro Nasdaq) Futures  

---

#### **1. Key Concepts & Definitions**  
- **Profit Taking:** Closing a position to lock in gains before price reverses.  
- **Fixed vs. Dynamic Targets:** Fixed (static price level) vs. dynamic (trailing stops, indicators).  
- **Risk-Reward Ratio:** Minimum 1:2 (e.g., 10 ticks risk, 20 ticks profit).  
- **Time-Based Exits:** Exiting after a set duration (e.g., 30 minutes) if target isn’t hit.  

---

#### **2. Calculation & Measurement**  
- **Fixed Target:**  
  - Set at key support/resistance levels or Fibonacci extensions (e.g., 1.618x initial risk).  
  - Example: Buy at 15,000, stop at 14,990 (10 ticks), target at 15,020 (20 ticks).  
- **Trailing Stop:**  
  - Move stop-loss to breakeven +1 tick after 1x risk achieved (e.g., 15,010 → stop at 15,001).  
  - Use ATR (14-period): Trail stop at 1.5x ATR below high.  
- **Indicator-Based:**  
  - RSI >70 (overbought) or <30 (oversold) to exit partial positions.  

---

#### **3. Trading Strategies for MNQ**  
- **Scalping (1-5 min timeframe):**  
  - Target: 8-12 ticks, stop-loss: 4-6 ticks.  
  - Exit 50% at 1x risk, trail rest with 3-tick stop.  
- **Swing Trading (15min-1hr):**  
  - Target: 30-50 ticks, stop-loss: 15-20 ticks.  
  - Exit 30% at 1.5x risk, 30% at 2x, trail remainder.  
- **Breakout Fade:**  
  - Exit longs if volume declines after breakout or price stalls at round numbers (e.g., 15,050).  

---

#### **4. Thresholds & Values**  
- **High Probability Zones:**  
  - Take profit at prior day’s high/low or VWAP ±1%.  
- **Time Decay:** Exit trades entering low-volume periods (11:30 AM - 1:30 PM EST).  
- **Volatility Adjustments:**  
  - MNQ average daily range: ~150 ticks. Adjust targets to 30-40% of ADR.  

---

#### **5. Common Pitfalls**  
- **Over-Optimization:** Avoid arbitrary profit targets; use market structure.  
- **Ignoring Context:** Don’t take profits in strong trends; use partial exits instead.  
- **Emotional Exits:** Stick to pre-defined rules (e.g., never move target wider).  

---

#### **6. MNQ-Specific Rules**  
- **Liquidity Zones:** Exit near round numbers (e.g., 15,100) where orders cluster.  
- **News Events:** Close positions before major Nasdaq-related news (e.g., AAPL earnings).  
- **Overnight Risk:** Avoid holding through 4:00 PM EST if target isn’t hit.  

---

### **Actionable Takeaways**  
1. **Scalping:** 8-tick target, 4-tick stop, exit 50% at 4 ticks.  
2. **Swing:** 30-tick target, 15-tick stop, trail after 20 ticks.  
3. **Always** use a risk-reward ratio ≥1:2.  
4. **Exit partials** at 1x risk, trail the rest with ATR(14).
