# Order Flow Bet Sizing

**Hunter Alpha’s Take: Order Flow Bet Sizing – Is This a Bet Worth Taking?**  

**1. The Edge: Where’s the Juice?**  
Edge is *asymmetry*—knowing something the market doesn’t price efficiently. Order flow is pure signal: institutional footprints, liquidity gaps, aggressive vs. passive volume. The advantage? **Predicting short-term momentum or exhaustion** before technicals catch up.  

- **Liquidity Sniping:** Big bids/offers getting eaten? That’s a tell. If a whale’s limit order gets hit and volume surges, bet on continuation until liquidity dries up.  
- **Delta Divergence:** More aggressive buying but price stalls? Smart money might be fading the move. Fade with them.  
- **Absorption Patterns:** Price rejects a level *with* high volume but no follow-through? Someone’s trapped. Bet against them.  

**Edge = Seeing the hidden poker tells in the tape.**  

---  

**2. How to Bet? Position Sizing Rules**  
Forget "how much to win." Focus on **how much you can lose** and still live to play the next hand.  

- **Variable Sizing:** Scale into high-conviction flows. Start small (1-2% risk), add if order flow confirms. No confirmation? Walk away.  
- **Liquidity-Based Stops:** Set stops where order flow invalidates your thesis (e.g., if absorption flips to panic selling).  
- **Asymmetric Payoff:** Bet bigger when flow shows *trapped participants* (e.g., sellers absorbed before a breakout).  

**Rule:** Never risk more than 1/3 of your daily loss limit on a single flow read. Variance is your friend, but bankruptcy isn’t.  

---  

**3. The Risk: Where It Can Go Wrong**  
- **False Flags:** Retail order flow mimics institutions. Learn to filter noise (e.g., spoofing, icebergs).  
- **Liquidity Blackouts:** Thin markets exaggerate flow signals. Betting on illiquid assets? Size down or avoid.  
- **Overleveraging:** Flow trades are short-term. Leverage kills when the edge lasts seconds.  

**Biggest Risk?** Falling in love with a thesis. If flow contradicts you *immediately*, fold.  

---  

**4. Evidence: Prove It Works**  
- **Academic:** Studies show order flow predicts short-term price changes (e.g., VPIN, volume delta research).  
- **Empirical:** Tape-reading traders (e.g., SMB Capital) live by flow. Watch time & sales in high-volume breakouts—see who’s chasing.  
- **Your Journal:** Backtest flow patterns in your market. Did aggressive buys at support lead to bounces 60%+ of the time? That’s your edge.  

---  

**Final Verdict:**  
**Worth the bet?** If you can:  
1. Read flow *objectively* (no hopium),  
2. Size for survivorship,  
3. Act fast—edge decays quicker than technicals.  

Otherwise, you’re gambling blind. **The tape doesn’t lie, but your ego might.**
